Welcome to Nohtyp, the furniture page

What you will find here?

On this page you can learn more about the materials of home furniture and inform yourself for your next purchase

What to consider

The first thing you should keep in mind are the types, nobody wants a piece of furniture that does not fit into their living room. The second thing is the type of material and the color of the furniture, both should fit well with the rest of the room. Finally, the durability of the material must be long, nobody wants to change their furniture once a year
